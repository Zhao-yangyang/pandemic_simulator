Advanced Strategy Techniques

üî¨ Vaccine Development Strategy

Vaccine development is the key long-term strategy for controlling pandemics. It's recommended to start investing in vaccine research early in the game, even if short-term effects aren't visible.

  • Prioritize basic research to increase development success rates
  • Establish multiple research centers to distribute risk
  • Monitor virus mutations and adjust vaccine strategies accordingly
  • Collaborate with international organizations to share research results

üè• Medical Resource Management

Proper allocation of medical resources is crucial to prevent healthcare system collapse. Balance is needed between different regions and various medical needs.

  • Establish tiered healthcare systems to avoid medical overload
  • Increase ICU beds and ventilator capacity
  • Train additional healthcare personnel
  • Build temporary hospitals for peak periods

üö´ Quarantine & Lockdown Strategy

Quarantine measures are effective tools for controlling disease spread, but economic and social costs must be considered.

  • Implement targeted quarantines, avoid one-size-fits-all approaches
  • Adjust quarantine levels based on outbreak severity
  • Provide necessary life support for quarantined individuals
  • Establish effective quarantine monitoring mechanisms
